Original: → Compressed: ( smaller)
⬇️ Download compressed audio (.webm)

Your browser re-encodes the audio in real time using its built-in Opus encoder — no upload, no external service. This means compression takes roughly as long as the audio's own length. Output is saved as .webm (Opus audio), which is small and widely playable, but not the original .mp3 format.

Your browser doesn't support in-browser audio recording (MediaRecorder). Try Chrome, Edge, or Firefox instead.

Ad playing while your video compresses…
Original: → Compressed: ( smaller)
⬇️ Download compressed video (.webm)

Your browser re-encodes the video in real time by playing it back and capturing it with its built-in encoder — no upload, no external service. Compression takes roughly as long as the video's own length. Output is saved as .webm (VP9/VP8 + Opus), which is small and widely playable, but not the original file format.

Your browser doesn't support in-browser video capture/recording (captureStream + MediaRecorder). Try Chrome, Edge, or Firefox instead.

100% client-side. Files never leave your device.
